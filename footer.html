How to reach temple:

If starting from Kottayam, Head East on Kottayam- Kumily road and if coming from Ponkunnam, head West to reach Kothala (12th mile) junction (21 km) Head North on Kothala-Eruthupuzha road for about 300 m and take a right turn. Head 200 m straight to reach the temple premises.
